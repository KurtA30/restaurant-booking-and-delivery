Restaurant Booking Rules

  1. Occupied, Out of order table and tables alloted for walk-in customers cannot be booked.
  2. Book the table At least 6 Hours before the intended meal time.
  3. 2 Hours is the maximum stay within the restaurant. There is no need to consume the 2 hours to give way for staff to clean and disinfect the used area.
  4. There are only 2 timeslots for each mealtime(Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner) to ensure that every table is thoroughly cleaned and safe to be used.
    Breakfast Lunch Dinner
    (08:00 - 10:00) and (10:00 - 12:00) (13:00 - 15:00) and (15:00 - 17:00) (18:00 - 20:00) and (20:00 - 22:00)

    Please see the restaurant schedule in the front page.

  5. Once you have booked succesfully, you will recieve a random code which should be shown to a staff upon entering the restaurant.
